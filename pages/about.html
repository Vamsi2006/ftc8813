About Us

The Winter Soldiers is a FIRST Tech Challenge team consisting of 14 students from Bettendorf, Iowa and the surrounding area. Since we started five years ago, we have evolved from a rookie team with a duct-taped together robot to a world-class team with an industrially designed robot.
